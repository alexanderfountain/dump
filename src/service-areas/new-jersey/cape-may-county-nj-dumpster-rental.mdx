##  Cape May County Recycling Services

Cape May County has it's own recycling program. They use a <a href='https://www.cmcmua.com/single-stream-recycling' target="_blank" rel="noopener norefferer">Single Stream Recycling</a> method to collect and sort all of the recyclable materials. This means that paper, plastic, metal, and glass are put into one container for pickup and curbside collecting. Acceptable materials include:

- Plastic items under 2.5 gallons
- Metal items under 5 gallons
- Glass containers less than 5 gallons

You are not to recycle:

- Food contaminated boxes
- Styrofoam packaging
- Polystyrene egg cartons
- Plastic bags
- Window glass / broken glass
- Beverage cups
- Shredded paper
- Paint cans
- PVP pipes
- Light bulbs
- Hazardous chemical or substances
- Wax-coated cardboard boxes

If you still have questions, or concerns regarding these rues, you may contact the CMCMUA Recycling Coordinator at (609)-465-9026.
