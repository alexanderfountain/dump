## Dumpster permits in Summit NJ
Summit New Jersey is fairly friendly with getting a permit for your dumpster rental. No permit is needed if the dumpster is going to be placed on your property. You'll only need a permit if you are going to be placing your roll-off container in the street. So long as your dumpster is not blocking a meter the permit fee is a simple flat rate of $35. If you are blocking a meter the fee is $35/day for one meter, if you are blocking multiple meters the fee is $25/day per additional meter.

You can fill out the <a href='https://nj-summit.civicplus.com/DocumentCenter/View/263/Dumpster-Permit-Request-Form-PDF?bidId=' target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">dumpster rental permit online</a> or call the Parking Service office directly at (908) 522-5100. Completed forms need to be printed and brought to Parking Services, City Hall, 512 Springfield Avenue, Summit. If you need any help filling out your dumpster rental permit you can <Link to={Paths.contact}>contact us</Link> and we'll be happy to help you get your permit.
