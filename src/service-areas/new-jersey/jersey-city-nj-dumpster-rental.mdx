## Jersey City New Jersey Recycling Guide

Since every city is different we have provided the specific rules that pertain to Jersey City and the recycling service. We have outlined them below for your preference:

- Curbside recycling is provided once a week which includes mixed newspapers, magazines, junk mail, office paper, telephone books, laundry detergent boxes and corrugated boxes. 
- Materials being recycled must be tied or in recycling containers no earlier than 7:00 pm and no later than 10:00 pm the night before scheduled collection day
- Do not put recycles in grocery store plastic bags, recycle these bags at your local Shoprite or Target
