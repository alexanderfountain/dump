Bridgewater Recycling Service

Recycling is picked up curbside in Bridgewater once every two Wednesdays. There are some specific rules to remember when using their recycling service which we have outlined below:

- Glass and plastic bottles, containers, etc. must be rinsed out thoroughly prior to recycling
- Newspapers must be bundles and tied with either strong or twine
- Paper and junk mail should be placed in brown paper bags or cardboard boxes and be placed to
the side of your bin
- Corrugated cardboard and pizza boxes must be cut into 2ft X 2ft squares and tied with twine
- Pizza boxes must be clean in order to be recycled. No grease.
