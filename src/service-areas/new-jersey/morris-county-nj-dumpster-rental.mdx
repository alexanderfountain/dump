## FAQs for Morris County NJ Dumpster Rentals

### What can and can't be recycled in Morris County NJ?

The Morris County Municipal Utilities Authority operates on an all-in-one, single-stream recycling system. This allows residents to combine all of the allowable recyclables listed below into one recycling container. They make your life easy and handle the tedious separation process, that is, as long as you do your part to recycle!

Allowable Recyclables in Morris County NJ:

- Mixed Paper, Files, Envelopes, Flyers and Newspaper
- Magazines, Catalogs, and Paperback Books
- Wrapping Paper
- Paper Bags
- Paper Towel and Toilet Paper Rolls
- Carton, Boxes and Corrugated Cardboard (flattened)
- Frozen Food Boxes and Clean Pizza Boxes
- 6 & 12-Pack Cartons from Soda
- Plastics labeled #1, #2 and #5
- Aluminum and Steel Cans
- Aluminum Foil (Clean)
- Loose Metal Jar Lids
- Glass Bottles and Jars (no lid, must be empty)

Do NOT Recycle the Following:

- Plastic bags, film or packaging
- Styrofoam
- Hangers (plastic, metal or wood)
- Food waste
- Wood scraps
- Plastic bottles/containers coded #3, #4, #6 & #7

### How can I dispose of hazardous waste and electronic items in Morris County NJ?

Morris County provides residents with a number of different hazardous waste collection events and resources to aid in the disposal of any hazardous or electronic waste. Morris County Municipal Utilities Authority typically hosts four different hazardous waste disposal events each year.

These disposal events are restricted to Morris County New Jersey residents and are held from 9am-2pm.

A full list of allowable materials can be found on the <a href='https://mcmua.com/sw_hhw_events.asp' target="_blank" rel="noopener norefferer">Hazardous Waste Events</a> page of the MCMUA website.

Recycling electronic waste is not permitted during these events. If you are looking for ways in which to dispose of your television, appliances, and other electronics, refer to the <a href='https://mcmua.com/materialdetail.asp?MaterialID=41' target="_blank" rel="noopener norefferer">Solid Waste Material Data Sheet</a> put together by the MCMUA. It outlines the specific locations you are permitted to recycle each of your devices.

### Where can I put a roll off dumpster rental in Morris County NJ?

In Morris County, dumpster rentals can be placed wherever you have space. That being said, there are some restrictions if you are planning on placing it on the county street. In this case, you may be required by Morris County, or your specific town, to apply for and display a permit.

If you are planning on placing your rental on your private property, like your driveway, you generally do not need to be concerned with permit regulations.
